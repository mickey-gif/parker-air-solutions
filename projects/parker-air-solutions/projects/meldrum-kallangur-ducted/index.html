Kallangur Ducted Install

Project Overview

Kallangur Ducted Install

A complete Daikin ducted air conditioning installation with AirTouch smart zoning for a family home in Kallangur. From ceiling ductwork and vent placement to outdoor unit pipework and zone sensor setup, this project delivered whole-home comfort with precise climate control.

Category Ducted Installation
Location Kallangur, QLD
System Daikin Ducted
Controls AirTouch ITC Sensors

The Brief: Whole-Home Comfort for a Kallangur Family

The homeowners of this Kallangur property were looking for a ducted air conditioning solution that could keep every room comfortable throughout the year. Like many Queensland homes, the existing setup left certain rooms sweltering in summer and too cold in winter, with no central system to balance the temperature across the house. They wanted a single, efficient system that would be invisible in the living spaces and simple to control—room by room if needed.

The Solution: Daikin Ducted with AirTouch Zone Control

After an on-site assessment, Parker Air Solutions recommended a Daikin ducted system paired with AirTouch ITC temperature sensors for individual zone control. Daikin's reputation for reliability and energy efficiency made it the ideal choice for a home of this size, and the AirTouch sensors allow the homeowners to set and monitor the temperature in each zone independently. This combination ensures that only the rooms being used are conditioned, keeping energy bills low without sacrificing comfort.

The Installation: Precision from Ceiling to Kerb

The Parker Air Solutions team arrived on-site and began by marking out and cutting precise openings in the ceiling for each supply vent. Getting the vent positions right is critical—it determines how evenly air is distributed across each room. With the openings prepared, the team fed lengths of insulated flexible ductwork through the ceiling cavity, connecting each run back to the central indoor unit. Working in the roof space in Queensland heat is demanding work, but careful routing and secure connections here are what separate a good installation from a great one.

Once the ductwork was connected and the ceiling diffusers fitted, the focus shifted to the exterior. The team ran refrigerant copper pipework along the outside walls, connecting the indoor unit to the Daikin outdoor condenser. Pipe covers were fitted to keep the installation neat and protect the lines from the elements. Finally, the AirTouch ITC wall sensors were installed in each zone, giving the homeowners fingertip control over the temperature in every part of the house.

The Result: Seamless Climate Control, Inside and Out

The finished installation delivers exactly what the homeowners were after: quiet, even airflow in every room, controlled zone by zone through the AirTouch system. From the street, only the outdoor unit and a clean pipe run are visible. Inside, subtle ceiling vents are the only sign that a full ducted system is at work. The Kallangur family now enjoys consistent comfort year-round, with the flexibility to condition only the rooms they need—saving energy and money without compromise.
