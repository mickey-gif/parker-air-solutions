Woodhill Ducted Install

Project Overview

Woodhill Ducted Install

A 16kW Daikin ducted system with AirTouch 5 smart zoning, eliminating hot and cold spots and delivering seamless whole-home climate control for a Queensland family.

Category Ducted Installation
Location Woodford, QLD
System 16kW Daikin
Controls AirTouch 5

The Problem: No More Hot & Cold Spots

Every Queensland homeowner knows the struggle: one room is freezing, while the next feels like a sauna. Old homes and new builds alike often suffer from uneven temperatures, making true, whole-home comfort feel impossible. For this family in Woodford, a traditional split-system solution with multiple units was not an option. They wanted a streamlined, unified system that could be easily controlled and blend into the home's aesthetic, not detract from it. The goal was simple: one system to rule them all, providing total climate control without compromising on style or efficiency.

The Solution: A Strategic Ducted Air Conditioning Plan

A comprehensive ducted air conditioning system was the clear answer. The process began with a detailed on-site assessment. Parker Air Solutions conducted an initial consultation to understand the homeowners' specific needs, including which rooms they wanted to heat or cool, and when. A team of three to four installers then arrived on the day of installation, ready to transform the home. The heart of the new system—a 16kW Daikin ducted system—was chosen for its power and reliability. This powerful system was paired with an AirTouch 5 smart zone control system to provide ultimate efficiency and control.

From Bones to Comfort: The Installation Process

The installation of a ducted air conditioning system is a precise, multi-step process that must be executed flawlessly to ensure long-term performance. It starts with the meticulous preparation of the installation area and careful placement of the indoor unit, which is typically hidden in the ceiling or under the floor. For the Woodford project, this meant discreetly integrating the system into the home's structure. Next, the technicians meticulously run the insulated ductwork—the veins of the system—from the indoor unit to each room, ensuring a balanced and even airflow throughout the entire house.

Once the indoor infrastructure is complete, the focus shifts to the exterior. The large, beige outdoor unit, or condenser, is carefully positioned on a stable, level surface outside the home, connected to the indoor unit by refrigerant piping and electrical wiring. The Parker Air Solutions team ensures the outdoor unit is placed with consideration for proximity to bedrooms and neighbours, and that the electrical connections are up to code. The visible vents and grilles are then fitted into the ceiling of each room to provide the final, seamless aesthetic.

With the physical components in place, the true magic of the system comes to life. The installation crew sets up the AirTouch 5 wall-mounted control panel, which serves as the "brain" of the ducted air conditioning system. This allows the homeowner to set temperatures and manage airflow to different zones, ensuring they only cool or heat the rooms they are using, which significantly reduces energy waste and lowers power bills. A final test and fine-tuning process ensures every vent delivers the right amount of air and that the entire system is running optimally before the final walkthrough with the client.

The Result: A Family Living in Perfect Climate

The Woodford project was a resounding success. The ducted air conditioning system now offers complete climate control, allowing the family to live in comfort no matter the season. Unlike a series of split systems, the ducted unit is largely invisible, with only a few subtle grilles in the ceiling, preserving the home's clean lines and modern aesthetic. The single outdoor unit saves space and looks cleaner than having multiple condensers on the side of the house.

The new system's benefits go beyond just temperature control. A modern ducted air conditioner with a smart zoning system like the AirTouch 5 offers superior energy efficiency by allowing the user to cool or heat only the areas they need. This means significant savings on quarterly electricity bills. A ducted system also improves indoor air quality by circulating filtered air throughout the home, which is a major benefit for families with allergies or respiratory conditions.

The case study demonstrates that a professional installation, led by a competent and experienced team, is the key to unlocking the full potential of a premium system. From the initial consultation to the final walkthrough, the process is streamlined and stress-free. The end result is not just a climate control system, but a solution that enhances a family's quality of life and provides year-round peace of mind. For the Woodford family, the investment in a ducted air conditioning system was an investment in a more comfortable, efficient, and beautiful home.
