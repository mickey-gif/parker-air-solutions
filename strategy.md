## Recurring Strategy

### Content (Every 3 Weeks — 4 cycles per quarter)
- **Shoot planning:** Adrian creates content strategy + brief (0.5h)

- **Shoot day:** Matt films on-site in Brisbane (4h) — 10 videos + photos per shoot
- **Video types:** Case studies, tips, testimonials, b-roll, before/after
- **Editing split:**
  - Matt: complex edits x6 — b-roll, case studies, multi-angle (9h)
  - Madison: simple edits x4 — trim, top & tail, talking head (3h)
- **Photo gallery:** Madison edits 10-20 images (1h)
- **Infographics:** Madison creates 2 per cycle (3h)
- **Blog:** Michael writes case study article with Claude assistance (1h)
- **Scheduling:** Kim sets posting dates + writes captions via `/kilr-copywriting` (1h)
- **Total per cycle:** 22.5h | **Quarterly (x4):** 90h

- Michael reviews, publishes, implements on site
- Adrian reviews keyword rankings and generates client report
- **Quarterly total:** 10h

### Strategic Notes
- **VO narrative ad** is a test format — if it performs well, roll out to other clients
- **Google Ads is net new** — start conservative, optimise from data before scaling budget
- **GTM is foundational** — once set up, all future tracking changes happen through GTM (no more editing site code)
- **Webflow → Cloudflare** unlocks faster page loads, better SEO, and full code control for Michael
- **Local services LP** gives us a dedicated conversion page instead of sending ad traffic to homepage
- **Skills/playbooks** are an investment — Michael can replicate GTM + tracking setup across all clients
- **SEO is Claude-powered** — Michael runs the skills, reviews output, implements. 10h instead of 20h.
