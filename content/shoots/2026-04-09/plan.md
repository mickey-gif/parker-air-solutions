## VIDEO 1: Case Study — Today's Install

**Type:** Conversion
**Talent:** Zac
**When to shoot:** Mid-morning, while install is happening
**Time on site:** 10 min

### Script

> **LINE 1 (Hook)**
> Zac stands next to the unit or the boxed unit before install.
> *"Today we're out in [suburb] — putting in a [brand] [X]kW split system. Here's what you're getting."*

> **LINE 2 (Size)**
> Gestures toward the room or unit.
> *"This is a [X]kW unit — right size for a room this size. Too small and it never catches up. Too big and it short cycles."*

> **LINE 3 (Key spec)**
> Pick whichever applies to the unit on the day:
> - WiFi: *"WiFi enabled — turn it on from your phone before you get home."*
> - Inverter: *"Inverter — ramps up and down instead of smashing power every time it kicks on."*
> - Energy rating: *"[X]-star energy rating. For a Queensland summer that matters."*

> **LINE 4 (Winter angle + wrap)**
> *"Getting this in now means they're not calling us in December when everyone else is. Clean install, right unit for the job."*

Film each line separately — cut together in post. If Zac fumbles, just re-prompt.

### Shot List

| # | Shot | Framing | Notes |
|---|------|---------|-------|
| 1 | Zac + unit on wall / box | Medium, eye level, static | Hook line — hand on unit |
| 2 | Unit brand label / model sticker | ECU, slow push in | Hold 3 seconds |
| 3 | Unit being lifted onto wall bracket | Wide, handheld follow | Install in progress |
| 4 | Zac gesturing at the room | Medium, static | Line 2 — pointing at the space |
| 5 | Refrigerant lines along wall | CU, low angle | Clean lines — shows quality |
| 6 | Zac delivering feature line | Medium, static | Line 3 — direct to camera |
| 7 | Unit powering on first time | Medium → CU on vents | **60fps.** First cool air. Money shot. |
| 8 | Zac — wrap line | Medium, static | Line 4. Winter angle. |
| 9 | Finished indoor unit — clean wide | Wide, low angle tilt up | Hero shot |

### Editor Notes
- Open on Shot 1, cut to install B-roll during Line 2. Text callout animates in for the feature spec. End on power-on then wrap line then hero shot.
- Text overlay: animated label for the key feature — e.g. *"WiFi Enabled"* or *"5-Star Energy Rating"*
- Under 60 seconds
- **Music:** Confident Hip-Hop / Modern Corporate | 95–105 BPM

## VIDEO 3: Why Does My Aircon Smell Like Wet Feet?

**Type:** Conversion
**Talent:** Jonah + Zac
**When to shoot:** While Zac is mid-service
**Time on site:** 2 min

### How It Plays

Zac answers without stopping work. Beats to hit:

## VIDEO 5: Pen Flip Challenge

**Type:** Algorithm
**Talent:** Zac, Jonah, Noddy
**When to shoot:** On a break. 5 min.
**Time on site:** 5 min

### How It Plays

Zac, Jonah, and Noddy each lined up with a pen and a cup.

**Text on screen:** *"If we land all three you're installing ducted this winter and beating the summer rush."*

Each flips their pen. Reactions. Miss or land — doesn't matter.

**The reveal:** At the end, show someone off camera dropping pens into the cup from above. That's the real footage. The whole thing was rigged.

### Shot List

| # | Shot | Framing | Notes |
|---|------|---------|-------|
| 1 | Three guys lined up with pens | Wide | Establish the challenge |
| 2 | Each pen flip — individual | Medium per person | Capture each flip and reaction |
| 3 | Pens in the cup (result) | CU on cup | Did they land? |
| 4 | Off camera hand dropping pens into cup | CU from above | **The reveal.** Film separately. |

### Editor Notes
- Text hook appears before the first flip
- Build tension on each flip — slow-mo optional

- Under 20 seconds
