## VIDEO 1: Case Study — Today's Job Walkthrough

**Framework:** Project Case Study
**Talent:** Zac
**When to shoot:** Mid-morning, while install is happening

### Full Script

> **LINE 1 (Hook)**
> Zac stands next to the indoor unit on the wall (or next to the boxed unit before install). Hand resting on it.
> *"Today we're out in [suburb] — we're putting in a [Daikin/Fujitsu/brand] [X]kW split system. Here's what you're getting."*

> **LINE 2 (Feature 1 — size/suitability)**
> Zac gestures toward the room or the unit.
> *"This is a [X]kW unit — that's the right size for a room this size. Too small and it never catches up. Too big and it short cycles."*

> **LINE 3 (Feature 2 — key spec)**
> Pick whichever applies to the unit on the day:
> - WiFi: *"It's WiFi enabled — you can turn it on from your phone before you even get home."*
> - Inverter: *"It's inverter — so it's not smashing power every time it kicks on. It ramps up and down. Cheaper to run."*
> - Energy rating: *"[X]-star energy rating. For a Queensland summer that actually matters."*

> **LINE 4 (Wrap)**
> Zac steps back slightly, looks at camera.
> *"Clean install, right unit for the job. That's what we do."*

*Note to Adrian: If Zac fumbles any line, just re-prompt. Film each line separately — they'll be cut together.*
